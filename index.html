Radial barchart representing the number of initiating and terminating transports.

Quiz 1: Any idea about the pick time in travel routine?

The Radial barchart shows the number of initiating and terminating transportation at each half hour. Thus, the answer can be read from the graph: it is 7-9am morning and 4-7pm evening commutes!
